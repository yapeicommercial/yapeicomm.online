Cookies Policy

Last Updated: June 2026 | Effective: June 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They help remember preferences, enhance user experience, and track website usage.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login information
  • Understand how you use our platform
  • Improve website performance and security
  • Personalize your experience
  • Display relevant content and advertisements

Managing Your Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Accept or reject cookies
  • Delete cookies from your device
  • Set preferences for specific websites
  • Enable "Do Not Track" features

Questions?

If you have questions about our cookies policy, please contact us. We're happy to help clarify how we use cookies to improve your experience.
