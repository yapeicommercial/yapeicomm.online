Introduction

Yapei Community Online ("we," "our," "us," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection when you visit our website at www.yapeionline.jo3.org and use our services.

By accessing and using Yapei Community Online, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our platform.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and username
  • Profile Data: Bio, profile picture, location, and interests
  • Communication Data: Messages, comments, posts, and support inquiries
  • Event Information: Event attendance, RSVP details, and participation records

Automatically Collected Information

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, click patterns, and features used
  • Cookies: Session data and user preferences
  • Location Data: General geographic location (with your permission)
Note: We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, or health data, unless expressly provided by you for specific purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Creating and maintaining your account
  • Providing community features (posts, comments, events)
  • Sending service updates and notifications
  • Responding to your inquiries and support requests
  • Improving our platform and user experience
  • Analyzing community trends and engagement
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring platform security
  • Complying with legal obligations

Data Sharing and Disclosure

When We Share Data

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist with hosting, email delivery, analytics, and customer support
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Community Features: Public posts and profiles visible to other community members as per your privacy settings
  • Business Partners: With your consent, for community improvement initiatives

Public Information

Your profile name, profile picture, posts, and comments may be visible to other community members depending on your privacy settings. You control the visibility of your content through account settings.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure password storage using industry-standard hashing
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Restricted access to personal data by authorized personnel only
  • Monitoring for unauthorized access attempts
Important: While we implement strong security measures, no online platform is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password.

Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Right to Portability: Obtain your data in a portable format
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your information

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@yapeionline.jo3.org.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 2 years after deletion
  • Communication Data: Retained for dispute resolution (up to 7 years)
  • Analytics Data: Retained for 12 months
  • Legal Data: Retained as required by applicable laws

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: info@yapeionline.jo3.org
  • Website: www.yapeionline.jo3.org
  • Location: Yapei, Ghana

We typically respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by email or prominent notice on our website. Your continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
