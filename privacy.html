1. Introduction

Our commitment to your privacy

Welcome to Yapei Community Online ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with our community.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site. We reserve the right to make changes to this privacy policy at any time and for any reason. We will alert you about any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this privacy policy.

By using our services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

Quick Summary

We collect information to provide better services to our community members. We never sell your personal data. You have control over your information and can request access, correction, or deletion at any time. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Legal Compliance

This policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable privacy laws. Your rights may vary depending on your location.

4. How We Use Your Information

Purpose of data processing

We use personal information collected via our website for a variety of business purposes described below:

4.1 Primary Uses

  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, provide customer support, and verify your identity.
  • Service Delivery: To provide, operate, and maintain our services, including community features, events, and content sharing.
  • Communication: To send you updates, security alerts, support messages, newsletters, and marketing communications (with your consent).
  • Personalization: To customize your experience, suggest relevant content, and remember your preferences.
  • Analytics: To analyze usage patterns, improve our website, develop new features, and conduct research.
  • Security: To monitor for suspicious activity, prevent fraud, protect against unauthorized access, and ensure platform safety.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

4.2 Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are from the European Economic Area, our legal basis for collecting and using your personal information depends on the specific context:

  • Contract Performance: When processing is necessary to fulfill our agreement with you (e.g., creating your account).
  • Legitimate Interests: When processing is necessary for our legitimate interests (e.g., improving our services, ensuring security).
  • Consent: When you have given explicit consent (e.g., for marketing emails, cookies).
  • Legal Obligation: When processing is required by law (e.g., retaining records).
  • Vital Interests: When processing is necessary to protect someone's life.

4.3 Automated Decision-Making

We do not engage in automated decision-making that produces legal effects concerning you. However, we may use automated systems for:

  • Content moderation and spam detection
  • Fraud prevention and security monitoring
  • Recommendation algorithms for community content
Your Choices

You can opt out of non-essential communications at any time through your account settings or by clicking "unsubscribe" in emails.

6. Data Retention

How long we keep your information

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6.1 Retention Periods

  • Account Information: Retained for as long as your account is active, plus 30 days after deletion
  • Profile Data: Retained until you delete it or close your account
  • Content Posts: Retained until you delete them or close your account
  • Communications: Retained for 2 years for customer service purposes
  • Usage Data: Retained in anonymized form indefinitely for analytics
  • Legal Records: Retained for 7 years to comply with legal obligations
  • Backup Copies: Retained for up to 90 days

6.2 Data Deletion

When you delete your account, we initiate a deletion process that removes your personal information from our active databases. However, some information may remain in backup copies for a limited time. Public content you've shared may remain visible if others have interacted with it.

Requesting Deletion

You can request early deletion of your data through your account settings or by contacting us. We will process your request within 30 days.

8. Data Security

How we protect your information

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, despite our safeguards and efforts to secure your information, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

8.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS technology (HTTPS).
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed and salted using bcrypt algorithm. We never store plain-text passwords.
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit internal access to personal data to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Monitoring: 24/7 monitoring for suspicious activity and potential breaches.
  • Backups: Regular encrypted backups to ensure data recovery capability.
  • Firewalls: Enterprise-grade firewalls protect our infrastructure.
  • Two-Factor Authentication: Optional 2FA available for all accounts.

8.2 Security Breach Response

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach
  • Provide information about the nature of the breach and data affected
  • Advise on steps you can take to protect yourself
  • Cooperate with relevant regulatory authorities
  • Take immediate steps to contain and remediate the breach
Your Responsibility

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials. Please use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication. Notify us immediately of any unauthorized access.

8.3 Security Recommendations

  • Use a strong password (at least 12 characters with mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
  • Enable two-factor authentication in your account settings
  • Never share your password with anyone
  • Log out from shared devices
  • Keep your email account secure (password resets go to your email)
  • Update your software and browser regularly

10. Children's Privacy

Protecting young users

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10.1 Age Restrictions

  • United States: Minimum age 13 (COPPA compliance)
  • European Economic Area: Minimum age 16 (or parental consent required)
  • Other regions: Minimum age as defined by local laws

10.2 Parental Rights

If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will:

  • Investigate and verify the information
  • Delete any personal information collected from the child
  • Terminate any account created by the child
  • Provide confirmation of deletion
For Parents

If you become aware that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately at privacy@yapeicommunity.com. We will take steps to remove that information and terminate the account.

10.3 Educational Use

If you are using our platform for educational purposes with students under 13, please ensure you have obtained appropriate parental consent and contact us to discuss specific compliance requirements.

Our website is hosted in Ghana and may be accessed from around the world. If you are accessing our website from outside Ghana, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in Ghana where our servers are located.

11.1 Data Transfer Mechanisms

For international data transfers, we implement appropriate safeguards:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses: EU-approved contracts for data transfers
  • Adequacy Decisions: For countries deemed adequate by European Commission
  • Binding Corporate Rules: For internal transfers within our organization
  • Consent: Explicit consent for specific transfers

11.2 GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Economic Area, we comply with GDPR requirements:

  • Data Protection Officer: Appointed DPO can be reached at dpo@yapeicommunity.com
  • Representative: We have an EU representative as required by GDPR Article 27

11.3 UK Users

Following Brexit, UK users are protected under UK GDPR. Our compliance extends to UK requirements with appropriate safeguards.

Your Local Laws

Data protection laws vary by country. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to Ghana and other countries where data protection laws may differ from those in your country of residence.
